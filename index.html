Platform features

Everything you need
to build and ship

🎨

6 Vibe Presets

Every theme ships with a full CSS design token system — colors, typography, spacing, border-radius, and button styles. Swap vibes instantly, everything updates.

📱

Responsive Preview

Switch between Desktop, Tablet and Mobile preview modes in the editor — changes the actual layout width, not just zoom.

⚡

Auto-save

Changes are saved automatically with a clear "Saved" indicator. No lost work, ever.

🔒

Secure Auth

JWT sessions with httpOnly cookies. Passwords hashed with bcrypt. Your account is yours alone.

View Analytics

Every published page tracks view counts, persisted in the database. Watch your audience grow in real time from your dashboard.

Auto-generated from your page title with collision handling. Share your page at a clean, memorable URL like /p/your-brand. Publish or unpublish any time with one click.
